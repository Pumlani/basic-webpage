Imvubu Nature Tours is a community-based tourism company established at Rondevlei Nature Reserve, Cape Town, in 2002. The reserve features a rustic but comfortable island bush camp, boat trips, guided nature walks, a lecture conference centre and outdoor conference centre. Employees are members of the previously disadvantaged local community and the company makes a great effort to source all labour and goods from the local community of possible. A percentage of profits is directed to the Zeekoevlei Environmental Education Programme (ZEEP) to promote environmental education, particularly for the surrounding communities of the Cape Flats.
